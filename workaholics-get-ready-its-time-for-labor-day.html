Workaholics Get Ready It’s Time for Labor Day

Tanto Irie - Island Vibes at La Playa Dayclub

Photo by La Playa Dayclub Media Team

Fort Lauderdale, FL - Workaholics La Playa Dayclub has you covered. This Labor Day Weekend the hottest new beach club in South Florida, La Playa Dayclub, invites you (21+) to play hard because we know you have been working hard.

Join La Playa Dayclub staff and other work and party-aholics at La Playa Dayclub this weekend.  With four days of entertainment and the best beachfront atmosphere, guests can unwind from a year of constant work and grind by enjoying a VIP cabana, $150 bottle specials, gorgeous views, and talented house music DJ’s to make you move and groove. To make it even better, La Playa Dayclub will offer free admission for all locals who add their names to the guest list.

“There is no better place to party on the beach in Fort Lauderdale,” says Brian Gallant, a long-time South Florida resident and local DJ & party goer. “This team built out an incredible club [La Playa Dayclub] that is right on the beach. On Saturday and Sunday they play house music, that's when the dance floor and the bottles really start flowing.

Here is what they have planned:

La Playa Dayclub kicks off the big Labor Day Weekend celebration on Friday, September 2, 2022, with their popular “Island Vibes” reggae party, featuring South Florida’s very own DJ Impulse Disco and the award-winning number 1 reggae radio host in the US, Tanto Irie! Featuring a brand new extended Reggae Happy Hour starting at 5 PM, guests can enjoy $5 draft beers, $7 Tito's cocktails, $9 appetizers, and $150 bottle specials all while they sit back, relax and enjoy the island vibes.

On Saturday, September 3, 2022, and Sunday, September 4, 2022, everyone (21+) is invited to awaken a sense of discovery as you step into the intimate jungle oasis of La Playa Dayclub.  Dance to FTLs hottest house music DJs while enjoying gorgeous beach views and the cool ocean breeze in your hair. Enjoy a VIP cabana and featured bottles for only $150 dollars. A lunch and dinner menu is also available from 11 AM - 10 PM and reservations are required.

On Monday, September 5, 2022, La Playa Dayclub will be turning up the music and the heat from 3 PM -10 PM with our resident DJ, Mas Inc, curating the vibe. Guests will dance through the day to the sound of house music and the ocean breeze as they take advantage of our VIP cabanas and $150 bottle specials.

About La Playa Dayclub

Awaken your sense of discovery as you step into the intimate jungle oasis of La Playa Dayclub, Fort Lauderdale Beach’s first true luxury day club experience. Nestled on the 2nd floor of Fort Lauderdale’s most beloved beachfront restaurant, Bo's Beach, this hidden sanctuary calls out to travelers, adventurers, and wanderers alike.

 

Dance alongside the setting sun, immersed in lush foliage and the rhythmic vibrations of the world’s best international House Music DJs. With our breathtaking views of Fort Lauderdale Beach, this paradise found is a haven for the daring voyager inside us all. To learn more about La Playa Dayclub, please visit our official website.
